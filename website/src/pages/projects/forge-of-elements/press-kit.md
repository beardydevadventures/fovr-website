## üß© Game Overview

**Forge of Elements** is a cozy VR puzzle-builder where players combine elements like fire, water, earth, air, and more to create fantastical worlds.  

Sculpt landscapes, experiment with hundreds of combinations, and unlock themed realms like enchanted groves and steampunk clockwork islands.  

Designed for both hand tracking and controllers, the game focuses on **relaxing, creative gameplay at your own pace**.
